Frequently Asked Questions

Are your vehicles street legal in the USA?+

Yes — every vehicle we import meets the federal 25-year rule and is legal for entry into the U.S. That said, state registration laws vary, especially when it comes to kei trucks and kei cars.

Can Kei trucks and Kei cars be registered for road use?+

Some states allow full registration, others only permit off-road, agricultural, or limited-use tags — and a few restrict on-road use entirely.

We always recommend checking with your local DMV before purchase. That said, we're happy to share what’s worked for past buyers and point you toward reputable third-party services that specialize in alternative registration options.
