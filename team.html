Two turbos are always better than one.
Thomas Muller
Founder & Owner
info@lethal30imports.com
Thomas has been wrenching since most people were still figuring out how to change a tire. At 16, he landed an internship as a rally crew chief and spent his teenage years traveling the U.S. with a V8-powered Rally Ranger team. Since then, he's stacked over 14 years of hands-on automotive experience — from ground-level garage work to high-stakes military aviation. At 24, Thomas joined the Army and became a Black Hawk helicopter mechanic. He later deployed to Afghanistan in support of Operation Freedom’s Sentinel, earning a combat device for his service. That time built in him an unmatched attention to detail, discipline, and grit — traits that now drive everything behind Lethal 30. He founded Lethal 30 after seeing a major gap in the U.S. import world: too many shady brokers, too little transparency. His mission? Deliver ligament imports with total honesty — and actually be there, on the ground in person, hand-picking every vehicle. No auctions-only. No surprises. No BS. Thomas is obsessed with JDM classics and dreams of one day building out a black RX-7 FD. Until then, he’s chasing down the rarest imports — and making sure his customers never have to settle for anything less than exactly what they want.

John Paese
Sales Manager
john@lethal30imports.com
From high school hallways to shop floors, John and Thomas have been wrenching together since their teens. With over a decade of hands-on experience as an ASE-certified mechanic and years leading sales teams, John brings both brains and backbone to Lethal 30 Imports. He’s more than just the guy handing over keys. John handles sales, meets one-on-one with customers, and rides shotgun on curated import requests. Laid back but laser-focused, he's the type to crack a joke while spotting a paint flaw from 20 feet away. When he’s not deep in the JDM tuner scene, you’ll find him bashing RC cars, bingeing anime, or daydreaming about towing something ridiculous with an R34. Above all, he’s meticulous, honest, and all-in on the mission: bringing badass, legal imports to U.S. roads — one import at a time.
